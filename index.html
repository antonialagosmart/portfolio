lagoSmart Live Support System

This Live Support System is designed for lagoSmart to enhance customer service efficiency by generating support tickets for user inquiries and automatically assigning them to available agents. This ensures that each request is handled promptly and by the most suitable representative.

Users can communicate with agents in real-time through a user-friendly interface, allowing for quick resolutions and effective interaction. The system's streamlined process not only improves response times but also elevates overall customer satisfaction by providing tailored support that meets users' needs.

INEC VETS Learning Management System

This Learning Management System (LMS) is specifically designed for the staff of the Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC) in Nigeria. The platform provides an intuitive interface for users to access essential training materials, complete necessary courses, and receive grades for their progress. By utilizing this system, INEC staff can familiarize themselves with crucial processes, enhancing their skills and knowledge to effectively contribute to their roles within the organization.

Human Resource Management System

The Human Resource Management System (HRMS) was designed to streamline core HR processes for organizations. It provides comprehensive modules for employee management, leave tracking, payroll processing, and attendance monitoring, ensuring efficient and accurate HR operations.

The HRMS enables HR teams to manage employee records, automate leave requests and approvals, process payroll seamlessly, and track attendance in real time. With robust reporting and analytics, organizations can gain insights into workforce trends and maintain compliance with HR policies.
